June 2026 updates include redesigned Energy Plots for multi-model comparison, expanded lightning rendering with three visualization modes (icon, heatmap, cluster aggregation), lightning type filtering by discharge (CC/CG), and isobands for threshold visualization across parameters. It also introduces the Meteomatics MCP (Model Context Protocol) enabling direct AI agent integration with weather data.
